Introduction

This journal is an exploration of the question: How can wabi-sabi aesthetics influence the development of digital interfaces to promote emotional connection? Through this series of entries, we dive into how embracing imperfection, impermanence, and natural randomness, core concepts of wabi-sabi, can be integrated into digital design. This experiment seeks to humanize technology by creating interactive experiences that evoke a sense of calm, connection, and emotional resonance with users.

Note: You can toggle between different journal entries using the button with the journal symbol, and explore these concepts through each design.

Research into wabi-sabi revealed how imperfection, like the random ripple patterns in water, could humanize digital interfaces. I designed a site where users create ripples on click, symbolizing natural randomness and transient beauty.
